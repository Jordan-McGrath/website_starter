AboutHire


Jordan McGrath

Freelance Photographer

Hi, I’m Jordan, a freelance Photographer who likes to travel, have a local beer and snap some photographs. Originally from Manchester but currently living in Liverpool and working in the surrounding areas. Photography is a big passion of mine having 8 years or so under my belt, however my story did not start off with Photography.

'People often called us perfectionists, but we were not looking for perfection. We were looking for some kind of magic in the music.' - Paul Simons

The year was 1990, Oasis had just formed with the single Supersonic, John Major had just become Thatcher’s successor and a spunky Northern fellow had just been born… yes, it was me. By the age of 8 I acquired my first drum kit, which was sat in my attic for many year, it was only when I formed my first band at school I got those drums down and started to play them. Since that point the rest became history!
I now have a degree in Interactive Music and Studio Production, as well as this I am an avid drummer with 15 years of solid experience to my name. If you are interested you can check me out on Soundcloud here, where you will find songs I have wrote as well as other little music snippets.
